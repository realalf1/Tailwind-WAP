Testimonials

UIN Sunan Gunung Djati, or the State Islamic University of Sunan Gunung Djati, is located in Bandung, Indonesia. It is named after Sunan Gunung Djati, one of the prominent figures in the spread of Islam in Java. Established in 1968, it has evolved from an Islamic higher education institution into a comprehensive university offering various academic programs.
